About Me

I am a Grade 11 student in the Physics-specialized class at HUS High School for Gifted Students, with a GPA of 9.5 in Grade 10 and 9.4 in Grade 11. I have achieved an SAT score of 1560 and an IELTS score of 7.5.

My interest in engineering developed gradually through years of engaging with mathematics, physics, and hands-on problem solving. From classroom experiments to community activities, I became increasingly aware of how scientific knowledge can be transformed into real solutions that serve people and the environment.

This realization guided me toward engineering—a field where I can apply technical understanding to create sustainable, practical, and human-centered innovations. I see engineering as a path to combine logic with compassion, and creativity with responsibility.
